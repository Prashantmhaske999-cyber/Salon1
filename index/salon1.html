The Beauty BoxPhilosophy

"Beauty is not a luxury — it is a language. We speak it fluently."

Founded in 2008 by master stylist Meera Kapoor, Beauty Box was built on a foundational belief: that every person deserves to feel extraordinary. What began as a boutique studio has grown into a landmark of luxury beauty.

We source only the finest professional-grade products — from Kerastase to La Mer — and our techniques are continually refined through global training programmes. We don't follow trends; we set them.

Our 4,500 sq ft salon features six private treatment suites, a bridal preparation lounge, a nail atelier, and a wellness spa — all designed with one purpose: your complete transformation.
